Instructions to Clear Browser History

Open Internet Explorer browser

  • Open "Tools" Menu. And then click on "Internet Options" in that menu
  • A new windows pane will open. In "General" Tab find the "Browsing History" section.
  • Click on the "Delete" button in the "Browsing History" Section.
  • If you are asked to delete all the files then click on "Delete all".
  • After that open a new Internet Explorer Browser and open the link of IFMS in that browser window.
